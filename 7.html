SnipPi GUI

I didn't know of any built-in Raspbian software at the time other than using command line. I created the SnipPi GUI to make it easier for myself or anyone who needed it and having 3 options of capture.

User: gives you the ability to start the capture from wherever holding the left mouse button and dragging around creating a rectangle which is your capture area.

Window: gives you the ability to click on a window even if it's on task bar.

Full Screen: will take a screen shot of screen.

Don't worry about the mouse position as you will notice it disappear briefly for the picture.
